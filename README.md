# Knowlix

### Preserving Knowledge Across Generations of Students

Knowlix is a modern educational resource-sharing platform that enables students, seniors, alumni, and institutions to collaboratively build a structured academic knowledge base.

Instead of valuable notes, question banks, and study materials disappearing every year when students graduate, Knowlix preserves and organizes them for future batches through a verified and moderated ecosystem.

---

## ✨ Why Knowlix?

Every academic batch creates valuable resources:

* Class notes
* Practice materials
* Previous year questions
* Model questions
* Assignments
* Presentations
* Study guides

Unfortunately, most of these resources are lost over time.

**Knowlix exists to ensure knowledge never graduates.**

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🔐 Institution-Verified Access

Only students from approved institutions can create accounts.

Example:

```text
Ankuram Academy
→ @student.ankuram.edu.np
```

This helps maintain authenticity, trust, and quality.

---

### 📚 Resource Library

Upload and discover:

* Notes
* Question Banks
* Model Questions
* Previous Year Papers
* Assignments
* Lab Reports
* Presentations
* Books
* Study Guides

---

### 🔎 Smart Search & Discovery

Find resources instantly using:

* Subject
* Grade/Class
* Institution
* Resource Type
* Academic Year
* Tags

---

### ⭐ Community-Driven Quality

Students can:

* Rate resources
* Comment on materials
* Ask questions
* Bookmark resources
* Follow contributors

The best resources naturally rise to the top.

---

### 🛡️ Moderation System

Every uploaded resource passes through a review workflow before becoming publicly available.

Moderators can:

* Approve uploads
* Reject invalid content
* Handle reports
* Maintain quality standards

---

### 📈 Contributor Reputation

Reward meaningful contributions through:

* Reputation points
* Contributor badges
* Upload statistics
* Community recognition

## 👥 User Roles

### Student

* Upload resources
* Download resources
* Bookmark materials
* Create collections
* Comment and rate content

### Moderator

* Review uploads
* Approve or reject resources
* Moderate discussions
* Manage reports

### Administrator

* Manage institutions
* Manage moderators
* Configure platform settings
* Access analytics
